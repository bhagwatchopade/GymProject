About Our Gym

OUR STORY

gym and Fitness was founded in 2002 as a family owned and operated business specialising in supplying high-quality gym equipment at great prices.


Instead of being just another gym equipment retailer, our founders wanted to be the best in the industry and set their minds to doing so! Over the last two decades Gym and Fitness has grown into one of Australia’s largest online fitness equipment retailers, helping thousands of customers live longer, happier and healthier lives.


HOW CAN WE HELP?

We want to help our members go further, so if you have any suggestions about our clubs, classes, personal training sessions or anything else, please come in and talk to one of our team member or if you prefer, drop us a note via our online enquiry forms.


OUR PHILOSOPHY

To offer the best equipment, range of classes, knowledgeable staff and personal advice in a welcoming environment

About Founder

Life Coach & Fitness Expert | Founder & Director at GRITFIT | Focused on Building a Fitter India | 15+ Years of Health & Fitness Industry Experience.


As the Founder-Owner of GritFit, a Lifestyle Transformation Fitness gym at Sinhagad Road Pune, I am dedicated to building a stronger and fitter community every day. Over the last 15 years, I’ve gained hands-on qualifications and accreditations by shaping clients and enriching businesses, Focusing on Health & Fitness. I’ve been a National Level boxing champion, have worked with some of the best names in the fitness industry – including Talwalkars, Gold’s Gym, David Lloyd Clubs (in India and the UK), Fitness Mantra, MultiFit. While I started as a General Trainer and quickly got promoted as a Personal Trainer, Club Manager, Operations Head and more during my journey, the zeal to start my own gym drove me towards GritFit – a leading and first Lifestyle Transformation Gym in Pune.
